# AI-Based Idea Validation Engine

## Problem Statement

Many entrepreneurs and students invest significant time and resources into ideas without understanding their market potential, feasibility, risks, or competition.

Traditional idea validation requires:
- Market research
- Competitor analysis
- Risk assessment
- Financial evaluation

These processes are time-consuming and often expensive.

## Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-powered Idea Validation Engine that automatically analyzes business ideas using multiple AI agents.

The system will provide:

- Market Potential Analysis
- Feasibility Analysis
- Competitor Analysis
- Risk Assessment
- Revenue Potential Analysis
- Overall Validation Score

## Expected Outcome

Users receive a detailed validation report and recommendations before investing resources into implementation.
