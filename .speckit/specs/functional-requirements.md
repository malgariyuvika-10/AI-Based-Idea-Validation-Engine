# Functional Requirements

## FR1 - Submit Idea

User shall be able to submit:

- Idea Name
- Description
- Target Audience
- Industry
- Revenue Model

---

## FR2 - Market Analysis

System shall analyze:

- Market Demand
- Industry Trends
- Market Size

---

## FR3 - Feasibility Analysis

System shall evaluate:

- Technical Complexity
- Resource Requirements
- Development Effort

---

## FR4 - Risk Analysis

System shall identify:

- Market Risks
- Operational Risks
- Regulatory Risks

---

## FR5 - Generate Validation Score

System shall generate:

- Market Score
- Feasibility Score
- Risk Score
- Overall Score

---

## FR6 - Generate Report

System shall produce a detailed validation report.
