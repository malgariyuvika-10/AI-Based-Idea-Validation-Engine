# Problem Statement

## Background

Many entrepreneurs invest significant time and resources into startup ideas without validating market demand, competition, feasibility, or financial viability.

## Problem

Manual idea validation is slow, expensive, and often subjective.

## Proposed Solution

Develop an AI-Based Idea Validation Engine that automates idea assessment using AI agents and data-driven scoring.

## Target Users

- Entrepreneurs
- Startup Founders
- Students
- Investors
- Innovation Teams

## Expected Outcome

Generate actionable validation reports and recommendations for submitted ideas.
